BENUE TECH SKILLS INFORMATION

Benue Tech Skills is a powerful program brought by the Digital governor of Benue State, Rev. Dr. Hyacinth Iormem Alia, to empower Youths with tech skills. This program offers comprehensive training in various skills including digital marketing, e-commerce, and Front-end development(Which I am currently learning)

The Benue Tech Skills is empowering ten thousands youths in different areas of technology the training is currently going on both physically and online for people who are not close but who also applied and got shotlisted, under the leadership of Engr. Jeffery Udende, it is a transformative and wonderful kickstart

Through the training, I have already gained access to expect instructors, Mr. Angwa Moses, Faga etc who has been putting me through in the program with a supportive community of like- minded.

The program is structured to last for six month with certificates to be awarded at the end of the program, job are to be given to participants who qualified

Benue Tech Skill has a profound impact on my life, providing me with a sense of purpose and direction, I am most grateful to my Digital Governor, and my tutors Mr. Samson Attah, Angwa Moses and Foga Kater
